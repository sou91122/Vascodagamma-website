Brief Description

Zanskar Padam LamayuruValley might be in the remote corner of Ladakh, but it is one of the most appealing places to visit in the Himalayas. Zanskar mountain range offers the most exciting trek owing to its altitude, not to forget the stunning panoramic views of the mountains. The entire trip is of 17 nightâ€™s duration, with April to September being the best months to travel.

Leh is the starting point and then you move on to Padum for the Zanskar Padam Lamayuru trek. There are different routes to Padum, with several tented camping spots alongside the river of Zanskar. On day three, en route Kargil, you will be experiencing the spectacular glaciers while passing over the Penzi La pass that connects Zanskar Valley with Suru Valley. This drive to Suru is bound to be completely stunning.
