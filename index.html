Faq

1. Is it safe to travel amidst COVID-19?
Two factors will come into play here:

1. The area in concern is allowing visitors or not.
2. Whether you follow all the safety protocols issued by the WHO (World Health Organization)

At Vascodagamma, we ensure that travellers are fit to travel to the locations. We, together with our travel partners, would take all the necessary precaution (testing) before allowing people to visit these areas. If new circulars are released, we will provide the updated information on our website at the earliest.

2. How to plan a family get together?

It can be quite daunting to plan a solo trip, let alone a family get together. However, you can apply these techniques for efficient planning. You need to figure out the purpose of the trip and what interests your family members. Then you would need to find common ground, then decide the overall budget, mode of transportation, schedule or the number of days. After deciding on all these terms, you are ready to visit the location of your preferences.

3. What are the essentials I should have before travelling?
  • Lightweight outdoor clothing together with accessories (could be worn in layers, check if there are any specific guidelines regarding clothing)
  • Sweaters and jackets
  • Walking shoes
  • Underwear, towels, swimsuits
  • Toiletries
  • Backpacks and itineraries
  • Sunglasses, glasses, and their respective cases
  • Phone and its chargers (adapters) (Phone should have an app that can act as a travel guide, and linguistics guide)
  • Electric converters
  • Travel pillows, eye masks, and ear plugs
  • Medicines (painkillers, inhalers, and similar items)
  • Foreign currency (cash and cards)
  • Passport (for international travelling)
  • Visa (for international locations)
  • First aid kit
  • Sunscreen, hats, umbrella
  • Protective clothing (especially if the area is covered with forests)
